Games

List of games I've programmed using JavaScript.

  1. Psychic Guessing Game (Click here)
    1. You get 10 chances to guess what letter the computer is thinking of.
    2. Choose a letter from A-Z.
    3. If you do not choose the correct letter, the Computer will list what letters you have chosen.
    4. Each incorrect guess will decrease your total number of guesses available..
    5. Once you have exhausted all of your chances, you will lose the game.
    6. Win and you get bragging rights for a couple minutes. Best of luck!!
  2. Crystal Collector (Click here)
    1. Each crystal has a hidden value that will only be revealed when clicked.
    2. With each click, you will add the crystal's value to your total score.
    3. You will win the game if your total score matches the magic number, and you will lose if your total score exceeds it.
    4. In the words of Han Solo, "Good luck, you're gonna need it!"
    5. Once you have exhausted all of your chances, you will lose the game.
    6. Win and you get bragging rights for a couple minutes. Best of luck!!
